Our Mission

The Our Mission of Alohawai Farm is to be the leading quality local organic produce supplier in the North Shore and throughout Oahu. We will build community through education, food accessibility, and by being a model of appropriate land stewardship and sustainable organic agricultural techniques.

Alohawai Farm

Aloha is Love, Water is Wai. At Alohawai (Love Water), we put our Mana (good vibes) into every plant, bringing you excellent quality superfoods in Mokule'ia, Oahu. The farm is 1 acre, located along Farrington Highway as one heads to Kaena Point along the North Shore. Wanna get involved?
