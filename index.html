Our Mission

The Mission of Alohawai Farm is to be the leading local organic produce supplier to Oahu. We will build community through education, food accessibility, and by being a model of appropriate land stewardship and sustainable organic agricultural techniques.

Alohawai Farm

Alohawai Farm is the first Farmily Ventures location, in Mokule'ia, Oahu. The farm is 1 acre, located along Farrington Highway as one heads to Kaena Point along the North Shore. Interested in getting involved? Here are some ideas!
